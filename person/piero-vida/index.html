Renegade Riders

Sette winchester per un massacro is a 1967 Italian spaghetti western film. It represents the official film debut for director Enzo G. Castellari, after his uncredited co-direction of Few Dollars for Django. The film stars Edd Byrnes in his first "spaghetti western" alongside Guy Madison.
