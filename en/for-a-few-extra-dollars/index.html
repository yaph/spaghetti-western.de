Spaghetti Western

For a Few Extra Dollars

For a Few Extra Dollars, also known as Fort Yuma Gold and Die Now, Pay Later, is a 1966 Italian Spaghetti Western film directed by Giorgio Ferroni.

Starring: Giuliano Gemma • José Calvo • Sophie Daumier • Jacques Sernas • Nello Pazzafini • Benito Stefanelli • Antonio Molino Rojo • Furio Meniconi • Andrea Bosic • Riccardo Pizzuti • Lorenzo Robledo • Roberto Alessandri • Pierre Cressoy • Guglielmo Spoletini • Serge Marquand • Dan Vadis • Ángel del Pozo • Jacques Herlin • José Manuel Martín
Music: Gianni Ferrio • Ennio Morricone
Produced by: Edmondo Amati • Maurizio Amati
