A Genius, Two Partners and a Dupe (Italian: Un genio, due compari, un pollo is a 1975 spaghetti western comedy film directed by Damiano Damiani and Sergio Leone, who directed the opening scene.

Starring: Terence Hill • Patrick McGoohan • Miou-Miou • Robert Charlebois • Klaus Kinski • Raimund Harmstorf • Rik Battaglia • Mario Brega • Friedrich von Ledebur • Jean Martin • Miriam Mahler • Clara Colosimo • Benito Stefanelli • Renato Baldini • Fernando Cerulli • Roy Bosier • Mario Valgoi • Gérard Boucaron • Piero Vida • Lina Franchi • Furio Meniconi
