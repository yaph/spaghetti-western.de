Duck, You Sucker!

Duck, You Sucker!, also known as A Fistful of Dynamite and Once Upon a Time… the Revolution, is a 1971 Italian epic Zapata Western film directed and co-written by Sergio Leone and starring Rod Steiger, James Coburn and Romolo Valli.

Starring: Rod Steiger • James Coburn • Romolo Valli • Antoine Saint-John • Franco Graziosi • Rik Battaglia • David Warbeck • Memè Perlini • Maria Monti • Antonio Casale • Nazzareno Natale • Benito Stefanelli • Aldo Sambrell • Furio Meniconi • Stefano Oppedisano • Vivienne Chandler • John Frederick • Jean Rougeul • Rosita Toros • Conrado San Martín
Music: Ennio Morricone
Genres: buddy film
