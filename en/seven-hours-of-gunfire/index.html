Seven Hours of Gunfire

Seven Hours of Gunfire is a 1965 Spanish-Italian Spaghetti Western film directed by Joaquín Luis Romero Marchent.

Starring: Raf Baldassarre • Antonio Molino Rojo • Álvaro de Luna, Duke of Trujillo • Lorenzo Robledo • Manuel De Blas • Helga Liné • Gloria Milland • Rik Van Nutter • Adrian Hoven • Kurt Großkurth • Helga Sommerfeld • Cris Huerta
Music: Angelo Francesco Lavagnino
