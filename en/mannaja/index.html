Spaghetti Western

Mannaja

Mannaja is an Italian 1977 Spaghetti Western film directed by Sergio Martino. The main role, Blade, is played by Maurizio Merli. Other central roles are played by John Steiner, Sonja Jeannine, Donald O'Brien, Philippe Leroy and Martine Brochard.

Starring: Maurizio Merli • John Steiner • Donald O'Brien • Philippe Leroy • Martine Brochard • Antonio Casale • Enzo Fiermonte • Rik Battaglia • Aldo Rendine • Enzo Maggio • Sophia Lombardo • Sonja Jeannine • Salvatore Puntillo
Music: Guido De Angelis
Produced by: Luciano Martino
