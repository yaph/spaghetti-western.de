Spaghetti Western

Adiós, Sabata

Adiós, Sabata is a 1970 Italian Spaghetti Western film directed by Gianfranco Parolini. It is the second film in The Sabata Trilogy by Parolini. Yul Brynner takes over the lead role from Lee Van Cleef, who stars in the first and third films.

Starring: Yul Brynner • Dean Reed • Gérard Herter • Sal Borgese • Franco Fantasia • Andrea Scotti • Nieves Navarro • Salvatore Billa • Bruno Corazzari • Andrea Aureli • Giuseppe Castellano • Federico Boido • Stefano Rizzo • Calisto Calisti • Ignazio Spalla • Gianni Rizzo • Giovanni Cianfriglia • Ángel Álvarez • José Canalejas • Fortunato Arena
Music: Bruno Nicolai
Produced by: Alberto Grimaldi
