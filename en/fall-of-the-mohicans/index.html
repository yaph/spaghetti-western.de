Fall of the Mohicans is a 1965 Spanish-Italian Spaghetti Western film directed by Mateo Cano. The film is based on James Fenimore Cooper's novel The Last of the Mohicans.

Starring: Jack Taylor • Paul Müller • Daniel Martín • Lorenzo Robledo • Luis Induni • José Manuel Martín • Carlos Casaravilla • Rufino Inglés • Sara Lezana • Alfonso del Real • José Riesgo • Pedro Fenollar
Music: Bruno Canfora
Genres: film based on literature
