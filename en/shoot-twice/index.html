Shoot Twice is a 1969 Italian Western film directed by Nando Cicero and starring Klaus Kinski and Antonio Sabàto. The film is also known as Twice a Judas.

Starring: Klaus Kinski • Antonio Sabàto • José Calvo • Emma Baron • Linda Sini • Claudia Rivelli • Gastone Pescucci • Cristina Galbó • Milo Quesada • Narciso Ibáñez Menta • Gianni Pulone
