Sonny and Jed is a 1972 Italian Spaghetti Western film about a sheriff's relentless effort to stop a robber and his girlfriend. The film was directed by Sergio Corbucci and is noted for its music, scored by Ennio Morricone.

Starring: Laura Betti • Franco Giacobini • Tomás Milián • Werner Pochath • Telly Savalas • Rosanna Yanni • Susan George • Eduardo Fajardo • Herbert Fux • Dan van Husen • Álvaro de Luna Blanco • Lorenzo Robledo
Music: Ennio Morricone
Produced by: Roberto Loyola
Genres: comedy film
