I am Sartana, Your Angel of Death is a 1969 Italian Western film directed by Giuliano Carnimeo and starring Gianni Garko. The film is presented on some DVD reissues under its German title, Sartana - Töten war sein täglich Brot.

Starring: Gianni Garko • Frank Wolff • Ettore Manni • Sal Borgese • Renato Baldini • José Torres • Gordon Mitchell • Federico Boido • Giovanni Petrucci • Lorenzo Piani • Klaus Kinski • John Bartha • Samson Burke • Ermelinda De Felice • Jean Louis • Tullio Altamura • Fortunato Arena • Remo Capitani • Franco Pesce
Music: Vasili Kojucharov
Produced by: Paolo Moffa • Aldo Addobbati
