Heroes of Fort Worth or Gli eroi di Fort Worth is a 1965 Italian Spaghetti Western film directed by Alberto De Martino.

Starring: Edmund Purdom • Ida Galli • Isarco Ravaioli • Umberto Raho • Emilio Rodríguez • Francesco Sormano • Eduardo Fajardo • Mónica Randall • Rafael Albaicín • Tullio Altamura • Germano Longo
Music: Manuel Parada
