Dead Men Don't Count is a 1968 Spanish-Italian Spaghetti Western film written and directed by Rafael Romero Marchent.

Starring: Mark Damon • Anthony Steffen • Raf Baldassarre • Luis Induni • Dyanik Zurakowska • Piero Lulli • Luis Barboo • Barta Barri • Jesús Guzmán • María Martín • Guillermo Méndez
