Rita of the West

Rita of the West, is a 1967 Italian musicarello-spaghetti western film directed by Ferdinando Baldi.

Starring: Rita Pavone • Terence Hill • Lucio Dalla • Teddy Reno • Pinuccio Ardia • Kirk Morris • Gordon Mitchell • Fernando Sancho • Livio Lorenzon • Nini Rosso • Gino Pernice
Produced by: Manolo Bolognini
