Black Eagle of Santa Fe

The Black Eagle of Santa Fe or Die Schwarzen Adler von Santa Fe is a 1965 West German and Italian international co-production western film directed by Alberto Cardone and Ernst Hofbauer.

Starring: Brad Harris • Joachim Hansen • Horst Frank • Werner Peters • Olga Schoberová • Serge Marquand • Ennio Girolami • Tony Kendall • Joseph Egger • Lorenzo Robledo • Pinkas Braun • Helga Sommerfeld • Edith Hancke • Jacques Bézard
Music: Gert Wilden
Produced by: Wolf C. Hartwig
