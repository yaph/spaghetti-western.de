Boot Hill is a 1969 Italian Spaghetti Western film starring Terence Hill and Bud Spencer. This film is the last one in a trilogy that started with God Forgives... I Don't! (1967), followed by Ace High (1968).

Starring: Terence Hill • Bud Spencer • Woody Strode • Glauco Onorato • Victor Buono • Eduardo Ciannelli • Lionel Stander • George Eastman • Alberto Dell’Acqua • Luciano Rossi • Enzo Fiermonte
Music: Carlo Rustichelli
Produced by: Enzo D’Ambrosio
Genres: comedy film
