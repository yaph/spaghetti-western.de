Buffalo Bill, Hero of the Far West

Buffalo Bill, Hero of the Far West is a 1964 Italian spaghetti western directed by Mario Costa.

Starring: Gordon Scott • Mario Brega • Jan Hendriks • Catherine Ribeiro • Roldano Lupi • Ingeborg Schöner • Ugo Sasso • Andrea Scotti • Luigi Tosi • Franco Fantasia • Hans von Borsody • Mirko Ellis • Feodor Chaliapin • Jacques Herlin • Piero Lulli
