Requiescant

Requiescant, also known as Kill and Pray, is a 1967 Spaghetti Western film directed by Carlo Lizzani.

Starring: Lou Castel • Mark Damon • Pier Paolo Pasolini • Ninetto Davoli • Franco Citti • Lorenza Guerrieri • Vittorio Duse • Massimo Sarchielli • Renato Terra • Barbara Frey • Luisa Baratto
Music: Riz Ortolani
Produced by: Carlo Lizzani
