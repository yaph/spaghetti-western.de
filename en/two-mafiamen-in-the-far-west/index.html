Two Mafiamen in the Far West

Two Mafiamen in the Far West is a 1964 Italian comedy western film directed by Giorgio Simonelli. Its stars Franco Franchi, Ciccio Ingrassia and Fernando Sancho.

Starring: Franco Franchi • Ciccio Ingrassia • Fernando Sancho • Aroldo Tieri • Hélène Chanel • Mimmo Poli • Olimpia Cavalli • Aldo Giuffrè • Adriano Micantoni • Alfredo Rizzo • José Torres • Ana Casares • Luis Peña Illescas • Félix Dafauce • Ignazio Spalla • Enzo Andronico • Mario Brega
