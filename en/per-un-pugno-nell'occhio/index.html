Spaghetti Western

Per un pugno nell'occhio

Per un pugno nell'occhio is a 1965 Italian film. It is a spaghetti western parody of Fistful of Dollars and was also known as Fistful of Knuckles and For a Fist in the Eye.

Starring: Franco Franchi • Ciccio Ingrassia • Franco & Ciccio • Mónica Randall • Jesús Puente Alzaga • Rafael Albaicín • Tito García • Guillermo Méndez
Music: Francesco De Masi
Produced by: Roberto Amoroso
Genres: parody film • buddy film
