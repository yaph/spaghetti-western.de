Four of the Apocalypse

Four of the Apocalypse is a 1975 Italian spaghetti western film directed by Lucio Fulci and starring Fabio Testi, Tomas Milian and Michael J. Pollard.

Starring: Fabio Testi • Lynne Frederick • Michael J. Pollard • Adolfo Lastretti • Bruno Corazzari • Giorgio Trestini • Donald O'Brien • Tomás Milián • Harry Baird • Lorenzo Robledo
Music: Franco Bixio
Produced by: Edmondo Amati
