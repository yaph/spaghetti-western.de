The Man Called Noon is a 1973 film directed by Peter Collinson. It stars Richard Crenna and Stephen Boyd. It is based on a 1970 Louis L'Amour novel of the same name.

Starring: Richard Crenna • Stephen Boyd • Rosanna Schiaffino • Farley Granger • Howard Ross • Aldo Sambrell • Bruce M. Fischer • Manuel De Blas • Patty Shepard • Ángel del Pozo • José Jaspe • Ricardo Palacios
