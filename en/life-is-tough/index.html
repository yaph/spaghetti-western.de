Life Is Tough, Eh Providence? is a 1972 Italian-Spanish Spaghetti Western comedy film directed by Giulio Petroni. The film was a box office success and generated an immediate sequel, Here We Go Again, Eh Providence?.

Starring: Tomás Milián • Janet Ågren • Dieter Eppler • Gabriella Giorgelli • Maurice Poli • Renzo Marignano • Giovanni Cianfriglia • Paul Müller • Mike Bongiorno • Carla Mancini • Luigi Antonio Guerra • Arnaldo Dell'Acqua • Gregg Palmer • Horst Janson
Music: Ennio Morricone
Genres: comedy film
