Spaghetti Western

One Thousand Dollars on the Black

Blood at Sundown is a Spaghetti Western film directed by Alberto Cardone. The picture marks the first appearance of the character Sartana, played by Gianni Garko. It is not, however considered an "official" Sartana film.

Starring: Anthony Steffen • Gianni Garko • Carla Calò • Erika Blanc • Carlo D'Angelo • Sieghardt Rupp • Franco Fantasia • Daniela Igliozzi • Olga Solbelli • Gianni Solaro • José Calvo • Angelika Ott • Sal Borgese • Chris Howland • Gino Marturano
Music: Michele Lacerenza
Produced by: Antonio Morelli
