Man of the East

Man of the East is a 1972 Italian spaghetti western film directed by Enzo Barboni starring Terence Hill.

Starring: Terence Hill • Yanti Somer • Harry Carey • Dominic Barto • Enzo Fiermonte • Danika La Loggia • Riccardo Pizzuti • Claudio Ruffini • Sal Borgese • Antonio Monselesan • John Bartha • Pupo De Luca • Salvatore Baccaro • Steffen Zacharias • Giovanni Cianfriglia • Gregory Walcott • Luigi Casellato • Emilio Messina • Mike Monty • Furio Meniconi • Margherita Horowitz
Music: Guido and Maurizio De Angelis
Produced by: Alberto Grimaldi
Genres: comedy film
