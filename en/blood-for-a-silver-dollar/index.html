Blood for a Silver Dollar, also known as One Silver Dollar, is a 1965 Italian-French Spaghetti Western film directed by Giorgio Ferroni, written by Giorgio Stegani and Ferroni and starring Giuliano Gemma and Ida Galli.

Starring: Giuliano Gemma • Giuseppe Addobbati • Ida Galli • Pierre Cressoy • Franco Fantasia • Benito Stefanelli • Tullio Altamura • Massimo Righi • Nello Pazzafini • Luigi Tosi • Sal Borgese • Andrea Scotti • Alfredo Rizzo • Ignazio Spalla • Gino Marturano • Fortunato Arena
Music: Gianni Ferrio
Produced by: Robert Dorfmann
